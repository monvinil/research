Research Engine

Cycle 0 · awaiting first run

What is this? An AI-powered research engine that maps the landscape of businesses likely to thrive in the AI economy over the next 3–5 years. It applies classical economic theories as operational lenses — Baumol, Schumpeter, Jevons, Perez, Polanyi — to detect structural forces and construct business models from the data up.

The engine uses a construct-first pipeline: Agent A scans data (FRED, BLS, EDGAR, O*NET, web) and extracts structural signals. Agent C grades signals by economic force, structural advantage, and timing readiness. Agent B constructs business models per opportunity. Master synthesizes into a tiered landscape map.
