01 // The Agricultural Paradox

The food system isn't broken. It's just optimized for a goal we no longer need.

For 70 years, the mandate was clear: produce maximum caloric volume to feed a growing world. The industry succeeded brilliantly. But in doing so, nutritional density became collateral damage.

02 // The Market Shift

The incumbents are built for volume. We are building the intelligence for quality.

The consumer no longer wants more calories; they are actively paying premiums for better fuel. Three massive forces are converging to create a new agricultural category.

  • The Demand: $962B wellness food market. A generation treats the body as a system to be optimized.
  • The Catalyst: 23% of US households are on GLP-1s. Caloric intake is dropping; the premium on nutrient density is skyrocketing.
  • The Institutional Payer: 929 Medicare Advantage plans now cover Food-as-Medicine. Health systems pay the premium because nutrient-dense food measurably lowers downstream medical costs.
03 // The Architecture

An intelligence company that farms to learn.

We build proprietary datasets mapping growing conditions to harvest quality. We feed that closed loop into an edge-native Bayesian optimization engine.

01. Sense

Hardware agnostic. We use commercial off-the-shelf edge telemetry (Moisture, EC, PAR Light, Temp) to capture continuous environmental state.

02. Harvest

We pair the sensor data directly with UC Davis lab-verified chemical and macro/micro nutrient analysis of the harvested crop.

03. Learn

Bayesian optimization on paired datasets. Designed to learn rapidly from 50–200 specific biological cycles, not 50 million images.

04 // Defensibility & IP

The physics-gated time moat.

In an era of LLM wrappers, our primary intellectual property is protected by physics and capital costs, not just lawyers.

  • The Data Moat (Trade Secrets): Every paired data point requires a physical 7–90 day production cycle and a $200–$400 UC Davis lab test. It cannot be scraped, synthesized, or crowdsourced. Replicating our first 200 cycles requires 18 months of physical time.
  • The Ecosystem Leverage: Strategic partnerships with the BFA (Bionutrient Food Association) and NDI. We receive data definitions and credibility, replacing $200–500K in standard-building costs for under $5K.
  • The Market Moat (Trademarks): We publish the methodology to build trust, but we aggressively protect the "Century Score" and "Century Verified" trademarks to capture value.
